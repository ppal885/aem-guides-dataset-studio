## UAC Grounding Contract
- Cite current Jira fields or similar Jira keys for every risk and scenario.
- Reject generic QA language unless tied to a concrete entity, output, component, customer, or historical Jira.
- Prefer compact bullets and scenario blocks over prose.
- Do not invent customers, fixes, environments, builds, or Jira keys.
- Do not produce a generic QA checklist; select only AEM Guides risks that are present in the evidence.
